## When To Use
- Retrieval context for fundraising deck flow.
- Seed, Series A, or growth narrative where thesis and proof must align.

## When Not To Use
- Internal operating review.
- Board governance update.

## Good Structure
- Slide 1: thesis summary generated last.
- Slide 2: category timing.
- Slide 3: painful workflow or unmet need.
- Slide 4: target segment and wedge.
- Slide 5: product demo flow.
- Slide 6: traction and usage quality.
- Slide 7: business model or unit economics.
- Slide 8: go-to-market roadmap.
- Slide 9: team or unfair advantage.
- Slide 10: ask and milestones.

## Anti Patterns
- Logo wall before thesis.
- Feature tour before pain.
- Market size with no reachable wedge.

## Evidence Requirements
- Customer evidence.
- Traction metric.
- Market source.
- Financial assumptions.

## Suggested Visual Components
- executive-summary

- product-demo-flow
- metric-system

- roadmap

## QA Checklist
- First three slides prove investor timing.
- Product proof supports business model proof.
- Ask maps to next milestones.
