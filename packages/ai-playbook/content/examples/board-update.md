## When To Use
- Retrieval context for board or investor update.
- Quarterly governance flow.

## When Not To Use
- Sales proposal.
- Product keynote.

## Good Structure
- Slide 1: board summary and decisions needed.
- Slide 2: actuals versus plan.
- Slide 3: growth and retention drivers.
- Slide 4: margin or cash bridge.
- Slide 5: strategic initiative progress.
- Slide 6: top risks and mitigations.
- Slide 7: decisions and asks.

## Anti Patterns
- Metrics without variance explanation.
- Bad news buried late.
- Operational detail in main flow.

## Evidence Requirements
- Actuals versus plan.
- Metric definitions.
- Risk register.
- Decision ask details.

## Suggested Visual Components
- executive-summary
- metric-system
- waterfall
- risk-matrix
- decision-log

## Example Markup
```html
<section class="slide" data-motion="static" data-visual="executive-summary">
  <h1>Growth is on plan, but the board should decide whether to protect margin or accelerate share capture</h1>
</section>
```

## QA Checklist
- Decisions needed are visible early.
- Variance has driver-level explanation.
- Risk movement since last meeting is clear.
