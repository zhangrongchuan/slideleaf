## When To Use
- SaaS, CRM, workflow, and operational tool decks.
- Enterprise buyer or internal strategy deck where repeated scanning matters.
- Dense but calm product analysis.

## When Not To Use
- Big consumer brand launch.
- Cinematic founder keynote.
- Deck needing expressive illustration.

## Good Structure
- Use neutral surfaces, clear sections, and compact panels.
- Prioritize scanning, comparison, and repeated decision workflows.
- Use icons and labels sparingly.
- Keep cards shallow and functional.

## Anti Patterns
- Marketing-style hero sections for an operating tool.
- Oversized decorative cards.
- One-note blue or purple palette.
- Vague interface chrome.

## Evidence Requirements
- Product claims should connect to workflow or operational metric evidence.

## Suggested Visual Components
- metric-system
- comparison-table
- product-demo-flow
- roadmap

## QA Checklist
- Layout supports scanning.
- UI elements feel functional, not decorative.
- Palette uses more than one hue family but remains restrained.
- Density is controlled.
